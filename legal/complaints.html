Complaints

How to raise a concern with us, and what happens next.

Please contact Propential to notify us if you are dissatisfied with our services or products.

How to tell us

You can tell us by:

  • Email: care@propential.com.au
  • Telephone: 1800 012 795
  • In writing to: 264 George Street, Sydney NSW 2000

What happens next

When the complaint is received, Propential staff will try to resolve your complaint immediately. If this is not possible, Propential will tell you who will be handling your complaint, their contact details, and the expected resolution date.

We can usually resolve your complaint within 5 business days. If we need to investigate further and thus need more time, we will advise you of the expected resolution date and keep you updated on our progress. During this period further information may be requested from you to assist with our investigation.

If you're not satisfied

If you do not agree with our decision or handling of your complaint, or you are unsatisfied with the Propential response, you can contact the Australian Financial Complaints Authority (AFCA) who will complete an independent review of our decision:

  • Online: www.afca.org.au
  • Email: info@afca.org.au
  • Phone: 1800 931 678
  • Mail: Australian Financial Complaints Authority, GPO Box 3, Melbourne VIC 3001

If you are concerned about your financial position and feel that you are in financial hardship, please contact us on the above details. Information on the Propential Financial Hardship policy can be found here.
